Article Pages Rendering Platform Re-architecture - [ January 2021 - March 2022 ]

Industry Digital Newspaper
Relevant Context Create a new web rendering platform responsible to render the article pages of the digital newspaper. The main purpose was to standardize the data layer, simplify the rendering logic (auto-scalable, shareable and resilient infrastructure to generate, cache, and serve the pre-rendered article completely from Cloud-front) improve the resiliency of the platform and overall improve the newspaper members experience
Challenges
  • Highly technical project which required to understand all the discussions and concepts across the engineering teams
  • Multiple stakeholders involved with different interests, goals and competing priorities. We had to constantly get agreements across the organization
  • Had to play the product manager role prioritizing and picking up the requirements
  • Since this project was a new prototype that aimed to change a platform that had remained unchanged, the team had to demonstrate its value by delivering small POC increments to the stakeholders
Outcomes
  • The new platform was launched gradually to production significantly improving the Cover Web Vitals and Ad Vitals performance over the legacy rendering platform
  • The 500 errors shown to the user is drastically lower and demonstrated to be resilient after mimicking a catastrophic failure as it continued serving articles to the end users
Tools and Techniques
  • Project Management Framework/Method: Kanban Method
  • Capacity Planning
  • Metrics Generation and tracking: Throughput, cycle time, lead time
  • Burn up charts
  • Roadmap
  • Internal Team’s working agreements
  • Project executive status reports
  • Jira - Project Management Tool
  • Google Data Studio for status and metrics reporting
  • Technical Decisions and Discussions tracker

Talk 2020 - [ April 2020 - December 2020 ]

Industry Digital Newspaper
Relevant Context This project aimed to implement a machine-learning-experimental tool that allowed the newspaper readers to search a database of transcripts and find all the US 2020 presidential candidates’ statements on different issues. This project started in early July 2020 and needed to be on time for the first presidential debate by September 29th 2020. It had to be done in collaboration with AWS engineers, since it was sponsored by them, to test one of their newest machine learning products: Kendra
Challenges
  • Pretty tight deadline
  • Recruited and formed a team from scratch
  • At early stages the project was just a concept with a few sketches
  • Synchronize AWS and the newspaper engineering teams making sure to make it work despite the different styles of work and organizational culture
Outcomes
  • Due to the tight deadline, created a very efficient system to onboard the new team members as the team continued building the solution in parallel
  • Coached and helped organize the work and communications across the engineering teams, the product manager and the stakeholders towards the end goal
  • Delivered the product on time and as expected for the first presidential debate
Tools and Techniques
  • Project Management Framework/Method: Kanban Method
  • Capacity Planning
  • Metrics Generation and tracking: Throughput, cycle time, lead time
  • Burn up charts
  • Roadmap
  • Internal Team’s working agreements
  • Project executive status reports
  • Jira - Project Management Tool
  • Google Data Studio for status and metrics reporting

Agile Coaching Activities - [ July 2021 - March 2022 ]

Industry Digital Newspaper
Relevant Context Ran various Agile maturity assessments on different teams. The process started with an awareness session to help them understand the purpose of the exercise which was not about evaluating or comparing the team but as a way to help them identify strengths, opportunities of improvement and gaps using the different Lean and Agile principles as a base and followed by shadowing activities, interviews and finally delivered a list of observations and suggestions. After that, we accompanied the teams on the process improvement steps
Challenges
  • Prevent the teams seeing the Agile maturity assessments as a way to evaluate or comparing them with other teams but as an exercise for their to continuous improvement
  • Keep the exercise as objective as possible with tangible results
Outcomes
  • Increased the Agile and Lean mindset and principles awareness of these teams
  • The teams welcomed these exercises and they improved the way of working
  • Overall it impacted the team’s morale, self organization and productivity
Tools and Techniques
  • Questionnaries
  • Interviews
  • Shadowing
  • Brainstorming and Retrospective exercises
  • Agile/Lean Maturity Assessment team and executive reports

Program to Manage a IT and SW development budget - [ Jul 2013 - Jun 2017 ]

Industry IT
Relevant Context This program was responsible to create and track the yearly IT and SW development (CAPEX) and maintenance (OPEX) budget of 120 applications portfolio globally distributed approximately with a cost of USD$100M
Challenges
  • Received multiple challenges from the corporation to reduce the budget and we had to find ways to reduce the impacts of doing it
  • Did not have a proprietary application to track the financials of such a big program
  • Had to distribute the cost of these applications globally by department and by tracking the apps usage
  • Each department and geography paying for their share of these applications continuously challenged the costs
Outcomes
  • Contributed with a USD$20M cost reduction and mitigated the impacts
  • Documented and automated various processes to create the yearly budgets and obtain the MTM financial reports
  • Generated comprehensive and customized reports to the executives and the departments and geographies paying their portion of the cost of these applications
  • Continuously discussed and negotiated with the departments paying their share of the cost of this portfolio
Tools and Techniques
  • Project Management Framework/Method: Program Budget Planning and Management
  • Financial Reports
  • BI Cognos Reports and IBM Forms Experience Builder
  • Advanced Excel Formulas and Macros
  • Negotiation
